# Afterlife Management System

## INSERT/UPDATE/DELETE Operations
1. **Insert New Soul**
   - Inserts a new soul into the `SOULS` relation.
2. **Insert New Deed**
   - Inserts a new deed into the `DEEDS` relation.
3. **Update Soul ID**
   - Updates the ID of an existing soul in the `SOULS` relation.
4. **Update Punishment Status**
   - Updates the punishment status of an existing soul in the `PUNISHMENT_ASSIGNED` relation.
5. **Update Residence of Souls**
   - Updates the residence information of existing souls in the `SOULS` relation.
6. **Delete Caretaker**
   - Deletes a caretaker from the `CARETAKER` relation.

## SELECT/READ Operations
7. **Perform Location Analysis of bad Deeds**
   - Retrieves cities with their negative deed percentage (bad deeds / total deeds * 100).
8. **Look at Caretaker Workload**
   - Retrieves caretakers along with the number of souls and number of punishments they are responsible for.
9. **Look at Caretaker Fairness**
   - Retrieves caretakers and their "harshness" score based on the duration of punishments assigned to a deed with a certain score.
10. **Calculate Life Score and Soul Score**
      - Retrieves a particular soul's life score and soul score.
11. **Look at unresolved punishments**
      - Retrieves unresolved punishments and their details.
